$$
\mathcal{P}(E) = {\huge \cup}F_{k} \quad \quad F_{k} = \{ A \in \mathcal{P}(E) \;;\; \left| A \right| = k \}
$$

comme $F_{k}$ disjoints,

$$
\left| \mathcal{P}(E) \right| = \sum \left| F_{k} \right| = \sum_{k} {n \choose k} = 2 ^{n}
$$
> attention, $F_{0} \neq \emptyset$
