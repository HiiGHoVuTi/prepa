# $\subset$

Soit $y\in S_{E}$, on a
$$
\begin{align}
\begin{cases}
L_{1} &: ay''+by'+cy&=d(t) \\
L_{2} &: ay_{0}''+by_{0}'+cy_{0} &= d(t)
\end{cases} \\
\end{align}
$$
$$
L_{1}-L_{2}: a(y-y_{0})'' + b(y-y_{0})'+c(y-y_{0}) = 0
$$
on pose $y_{h}=y-y_{0}$, $y_{h} \in S_{H}$ ok.
