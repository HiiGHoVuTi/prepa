Linéarité
---

Soient $y_{1},y_{2}\in F,\lambda\in \mathbb{K}$.

$$
\begin{align}
f^{-1}(y_{1}+\lambda y_{2}) &= f^{-1}(f(x_{1})+\lambda f(x_{2})) \\
&= f^{-1}(f(x_{1}+\lambda x_{2})) \\
&= x_{1} + \lambda x_{2} \\
&= f^{-1}(y_{1})+\lambda f^{-1}(y_{2})
\end{align}
$$
